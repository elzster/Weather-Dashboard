Latitude vs Temperature

In This plot, we pulled together a sample from our dataset taking the latitude relative to the level of temperature experienced.


Of all the factors taken in from observation, we did see the strongest correlation between temperatures experienced and altitude.

We see the trend that the closer you traveled towards the equator, the hotter the temperature experienced would be.
